lets make Roast Chicken

Roast chicken is a classic recipe, where a whole chicken is marinated with herbs, spices, and sometimes citrus, then roasted to golden perfection.
result is tender, juicy meat with crispy skin, often served alongside vegetables and gravy for a comforting and timeless meal.


Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken: 1 (around 1.5 kg)
  • Olive Oil or Butter: 3 tablespoons
  • Garlic: 4 cloves (minced)
  • Lemon: 1 (halved)
  • Herbs: Fresh rosemary, thyme, or parsley (chopped)
  • Spices: Paprika, black pepper, and salt
  • Vegetables (optional): Potatoes, carrots, and onions (chopped for roasting alongside)

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200째C (400째F).
  2. Clean the chicken and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Rub the chicken with olive oil or butter, minced garlic, spices, and chopped herbs.
  4. Stuff the cavity with a halved lemon and additional herbs for flavor.
  5. Place the chicken on a baking tray. Add chopped vegetables around it if desired.
  6. Roast in the oven for 1 to 1.5 hours, basting occasionally with the juices.
  7. Check if the chicken is done by inserting a thermometer into the thickest part (should read 75째C/165째F).
  8. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve warm.
