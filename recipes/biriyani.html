Lets make Biriyani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made by layering spiced rice with marinated meat, vegetables, or seafood. The rich blend of spices, including turmeric, cardamom, and cloves,infuses the dish with a unique aroma and taste. Often garnished with fried onions, nuts, and raisins, biryani is a centerpiece of celebratory meals across South Asia.

Ingredients

  • Rice: 2 cups (Basmati preferred)
  • RMeat or Vegetables: 500g (chicken, mutton, or vegetables)
  • ogurt: 1 cup
  • Onions: 2 large (sliced and fried)
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium (chopped)
  • Spices: Bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, cumin seeds,
    turmeric, red chili powder
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh Herbs: Coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
  • Oil or Ghee: 4 tablespoons

Recipe Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Cook it until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Marinate the meat or vegetables with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  3. In a large pan, heat oil or ghee and fry onions until golden brown. Remove and set aside for garnishing.
  4. Saut√© the marinated meat or vegetables in the same pan until partially cooked.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, spices, and fresh herbs. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  6. In a pot, layer the cooked rice and meat mixture. Sprinkle fried onions, saffron milk, and herbs on top.
  7. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-30 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or chutney.
